LabelFunctions::Click (deprecated for: GenericObject Click)

WR
Attempts to perform a standard Click on the Label

Attempts to perform a standard Click on the Object.

Attempts to click on the Label that is indicated in the map file by the Step command. This should provide for a click on any type of Label.

Fields: [ ]=Optional with Default Value

    Examples:

    • T, AWindow, ALabel, CLICK
      Attempts to perform a standard Label Click on ALabel in AWindow.

      Attempts to click on ALabel in AWindow as defined in the current App Map. This should provide for a click on any type of Label object.
